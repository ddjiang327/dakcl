Color Trainer

A simple, powerful tool built for daily use.

What this app does

Color Trainer helps you improve your color perception and visual accuracy through simple, focused training exercises. It is designed for designers, creators, and anyone who wants to sharpen their ability to distinguish and understand color more effectively.

Promotional Text

Train your eyes. Improve your color sense. Build stronger visual intuition in minutes a day.

Key Features

  • Fast and lightweight training interface
  • Simple, distraction-free design
  • Color recognition and comparison exercises
  • Works fully on-device
  • No unnecessary tracking or data collection

Why Color Trainer

Most tools teach theory. This app trains your perception through repetition and feedback. The goal is simple: help you see color more accurately and make faster visual decisions.

Privacy

We respect your privacy. Color Trainer does not collect, store, or share personal data. All functionality runs locally on your device.
